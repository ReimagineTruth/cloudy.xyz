How to Sign Up

Step 1: Install Pi Browser

Download the Pi Browser from the Google Play Store or iOS App Store to access the Pi Network ecosystem.

Step 2: Log In to Pi Network

Open the Pi Browser and sign in with your Pi Network account credentials.

Step 3: Authorize CloudyPi

Click "Sign Up with Pi" to authorize CloudyPi to access your Pi account details securely.

Step 4: Start Creating

Once signed up, set up your workspace and begin using CloudyPi's tools!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Pi Network account?

Yes, CloudyPi uses Pi Network for secure authentication. Download the Pi Network app to create an account if you don't have one.

Is my data safe with CloudyPi?

Absolutely. We use Pi Network's secure authentication and prioritize your privacy with robust data protection.

Can I sign up without Pi Browser?

No, the Pi Browser is required for Pi Network authentication to ensure a secure sign-up process.

What can I do after signing up?

You'll gain access to workspace tools, analytics, and the ability to sell digital goods with Pi payments.
