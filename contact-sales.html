Contact Our Sales Team

Ready to see how Cloudy can transform your workflow? Request a personalized demo with our sales team to explore features, pricing, and integration options tailored to your needs.

Fill out the form, and we’ll get back to you within 24 hours to schedule your demo.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit the form?

Our sales team will review your request and reach out within 24 hours (business days) to schedule your demo via email or phone, based on your preference.

How long is the demo?

Demos typically last 30-60 minutes, depending on your needs. We’ll tailor it to cover features, pricing, and integrations relevant to your team.

Is the demo free?

Yes, there’s no cost to request or attend a demo. It’s a chance to explore Cloudy with no obligation.

Can I invite my team?

Absolutely! Let us know in the message field, and we’ll coordinate a group demo for your team.

What if I need help outside of a demo?

For general support, visit our Help Center. For urgent sales inquiries, email sales@cloudyapp.com.

Do you offer custom pricing?

Yes, for teams of 50+ or enterprise needs, we can discuss custom plans during the demo. Include your requirements in the form.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes, reply to our confirmation email to reschedule or cancel. We’re flexible to fit your schedule.
