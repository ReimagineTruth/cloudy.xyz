Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Free plan?

The Free plan offers unlimited pages and blocks, 5MB file uploads, 10 guest collaborators, and 5 Cloudy AI queries per month—perfect for individuals or small teams.

How does the 20% yearly discount work?

Opt for "Pay Yearly" to save 20% on Plus and Business plans. For example, Plus drops from 12 Pi/month to 10 Pi/month, billed annually as 120 Pi.

What is Pi Payment?

Pi Payments use Pi, a cryptocurrency from the Pi Network, offering fast, secure, and decentralized transactions. Learn more at Pi Payments.

What is Pi, and how do I get it?

Pi is a cryptocurrency you can mine on your phone via the Pi Network app. Download it from the App Store or Google Play, sign up, and start mining to earn Pi for Cloudy plans.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can switch plans anytime from your Cloudy dashboard. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed storage limits?

You'll be notified to upgrade your plan or manage your storage. Additional storage can be purchased separately starting at 1 Pi for 10GB.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

The Free plan is your trial! Upgrade to Creator or Team anytime to access more features—no trial period needed for paid plans.

How does billing work with Pi?

Billing is monthly via Pi Network. Ensure your Pi wallet has enough balance; payments are deducted automatically on your renewal date.

What kind of support is included?

Free plan includes community support. Creator offers priority email, and Team provides 24/7 chat and email support with faster response times.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing cycle—no refunds for partial months.
